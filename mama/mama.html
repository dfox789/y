Things Mama Never Said!

(Unless she wears Combat Boots)

I met a 20 year old woman who planned on entering the Air Force when she was 18. She took the entrance exams and scored high enough to qualify for any job the Air Force had to offer. Prior to entering she met a man, fell in love and put her goals on hold......Unfortunately this is Typical..... Fast forward 2 years; she is unhappy in a relationship, still living at home without a professional skill-set, was recently fired from a waitressing job, and has a drug problem.
